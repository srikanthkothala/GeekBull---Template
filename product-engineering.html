Product

Engineering Development Implementation and Integration Enhancements and Extensions Testing

We deliver robust, feature-rich and user-friendly products built across different technologies and environments. With our deep expertise across the product lifecycle from idea to market, and customer-centric approach, we can significantly improve your time-to-value and ensure a low cost of ownership.

Product Development

We offer end-to-end product development services from conceptualization and planning to deployment and support. With an agile approach to product development, our global service capabilities span across different geographies industry verticals.

Implementation and Integration

We offer end-to-end product implementation services which includes defining the implementation guidelines, creating a roadmap for customizations, and ensuring that different systems integrate and work well together. We leverage pre-built templates, frameworks, standards, and reusable components to accelerate deployment and deliver faster time-to-value.

Enhancements and Extensions

We work closely with our customers to understand their technology landscape and business challenges. Our product engineers help improve their existing products by adding new features and functionality, ensuring mobile capabilities to meet new market demands and stay competitive.

Product Testing

Due to rising competition, businesses are looking to deliver new products faster at lower costs without compromising on quality. We offer a full range of automated product testing services across the entire lifecycle across different technologies powered by robust tools, processes, and testing frameworks.

Product Support

We provide 24x7 support and maintenance, ensuring that your systems are available and performing optimally at all times.

Legacy Modernization

To meet changing business requirements, we can help you transform your legacy products cost-effectively and within short time-frames. Our product engineers ensure a smooth transition to new technology platforms by working closely with all the stakeholders and providing detailed product documentation and training to your staff.
